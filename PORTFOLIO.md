## Client Work

### 1. UNITAR International University

**Industry:** Education · **Location:** Petaling Jaya, Malaysia
**Services:** Performance Marketing, Reporting & Analysis, Content Marketing

**The challenge:** TikTok CPLs had hit RM525 and Facebook RM291 — top agencies had failed to scale without CPL inflation. UNITAR needed aggressive student acquisition targets met without ballooning costs.

**What we did:** Full-funnel messaging matrix across TikTok, Google, and Meta. Facebook CAPI integration for algorithmic efficiency. Channel strategy differentiated by role: intent-capture vs. storytelling.

**Results (Jun–Sep 2025):**

- 32,000+ qualified student leads
- **47% reduction in CPL** year-on-year, at RM4M ad spend
- **77% share of total impressions** in Malaysia's Education & Training category
